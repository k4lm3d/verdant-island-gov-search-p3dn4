About Our Island

Government of Verdant Island

Serving our citizens with transparency, innovation, and aloha spirit since 1962.

Our Mission

To foster a thriving, sustainable island community through transparent governance, innovative public services, and unwavering commitment to our natural heritage.

Our People

Home to 148,000 residents across 12 districts, Verdant Island celebrates a rich cultural tapestry woven from Pacific Islander, Asian, and European traditions.

Our Environment

With 340 km² of lush rainforests, pristine coastlines, and volcanic highlands, our island government is dedicated to preserving 60% of land as protected reserves.
